No matter if you aim to build a new startup, develop your product or implement AI, you are probably considering two options of covering the tech side of your business: building your internal team of developers or outsourcing.

Both options are bad and here is why:
Building an internal team is expensive
It costs $31,970 to hire one developer, according to PayScale. This includes internal and external costs such as in-house recruiting staff, office work, advertising costs, third-party fees, relocation fees, and training.
$31,970To hire one developer
Hiring developers is time-consuming
It takes 42 days on average to fill a new tech position, according to WantedAnalytics. Apart from the time and money aspect, you are facing rigid competition in the tech talent pool – competing with tech giants such as Google and Facebook.
42 DAYSTo fill new tech position
Outsourcing tech is a high-risk commitment
Traditional outsourcing companies often focus on delivering tasks rather than commitment to product success. The traditional outsourcing implies remote work, and it may cause friction in the process if not conducted properly.
